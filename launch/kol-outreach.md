# KOL outreach

The DM templates for individual journalists and creators are kept off the public repo on purpose — they name specific people and we'd rather not have those templates indexed by search engines.

Maintainers: the templates live alongside this repo in `anyfn-kol-templates.local.md`. Reach out to the team if you need a copy.

## What stays public

The shape of the outreach plan, since it's useful as a reference:

- **Target:** ~10 people whose beat overlaps anyfn — Android internals reporters, indie AI tinkerers, agentic-UX commentators.
- **Cadence:** send during the recipient's working hours, Tue/Wed only.
- **Content:** one sentence acknowledging their work, one sentence on what anyfn does, one link. No pitch deck. No "would love to hop on a call."
- **Follow-up:** once, after 7 days, with a single line. Then stop.
- **Reciprocity:** thank-you post on X if they cover anyfn. Always.
